Life CFO
Third-Party Risk Management Policy
Effective Date: 5 March 2026
Organization: Life CFO
________________________________________
1. Purpose
This policy defines how Life CFO evaluates, manages, and monitors third-party service providers that support the platform. The objective is to reduce operational, security, and data protection risks associated with external vendors.
________________________________________
2. Scope
This policy applies to all third-party vendors that provide infrastructure, software services, or data integrations used by Life CFO, particularly vendors that process, store, or transmit user data.
________________________________________
3. Vendor Selection
Life CFO selects vendors based on reliability, security practices, and industry reputation. Preference is given to established technology providers that maintain strong security standards and compliance programs.
________________________________________
4. Current Third-Party Providers
Life CFO currently relies on trusted service providers for core platform functionality, including:
•	Cloud hosting and deployment infrastructure
•	Backend database and authentication services
•	Financial data connectivity providers
•	Transactional email infrastructure
These vendors maintain their own security controls, encryption standards, and operational monitoring systems.
________________________________________
5. Security Controls
Third-party providers are selected with consideration for:
•	Encryption of data in transit
•	Secure authentication and access controls
•	Platform monitoring and infrastructure protection
•	Vendor security practices and documentation
Access to third-party systems is restricted using role-based permissions and multi-factor authentication where available.
________________________________________
6. Vendor Monitoring
Life CFO periodically reviews vendor services and security practices. If a vendor experiences a security incident or no longer meets operational requirements, alternatives may be evaluated.
________________________________________
7. Data Protection
Third-party vendors only receive access to the minimum data necessary to perform their function. Life CFO does not share user data with vendors for marketing or unrelated purposes.
________________________________________
8. Policy Review
This policy is reviewed periodically and updated as the Life CFO platform evolves.
