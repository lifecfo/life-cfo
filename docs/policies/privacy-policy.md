1. Introduction
Life CFO (“we,” “us,” or “our”) provides a financial decision-support application designed to help households manage financial information and make informed decisions.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information, including financial data accessed through Plaid Inc. (“Plaid”).
________________________________________
2. Information We Collect
2.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we may collect:
•	Name
•	Email address
•	Authentication credentials
Authentication is managed through secure third-party infrastructure.
________________________________________
2.2 Financial Information (via Plaid)
If you choose to connect financial accounts, we use Plaid to access certain financial data on your behalf.
Through Plaid, we may receive:
•	Account balances
•	Transaction history
•	Account metadata (e.g., institution name, account type)
We do not collect or store your banking login credentials.
Your financial credentials are entered directly into Plaid Link and are not visible to or stored by Life CFO.
Plaid’s use of your information is governed by Plaid’s Privacy Policy:
https://plaid.com/legal
________________________________________
3. How We Use Your Information
We use collected information to:
•	Provide financial insights and analysis
•	Display account and transaction information
•	Improve application functionality
•	Ensure platform security
•	Comply with legal obligations
We do not sell personal data.
We do not use financial data for advertising purposes.
________________________________________
4. Data Storage & Security
Life CFO uses reputable cloud service providers, including:
•	Vercel (application hosting)
•	Supabase (database and authentication)
•	Plaid (financial data aggregation)
Encryption
•	All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher.
•	Data stored in our database is encrypted at rest by our infrastructure provider.
Access to production systems is restricted and protected with multi-factor authentication (MFA).
